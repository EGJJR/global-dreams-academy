Global Dreams Academy
A Youth Basketball Development League and Training Program by Luo Lakeside Group LLC

About Luo Lakeside Group LLC
Luo Lakeside Group LLC is a youth development organization that uses sport as a platform for skill development, leadership growth, and community engagement. The organization is committed to creating intentional, development-first athletic environments that support young people both on and off the court.

Origin Story: Why Global Dreams Academy?
Global Dreams Academy was created to reflect the unique identity of Berrien Springs – a small town with a global heartbeat. Influenced by the international presence of Andrews University and a strong multicultural community, Berrien Springs is home to families and young athletes from diverse backgrounds. Global Dreams Academy exists to honor those stories while providing a structured, high-quality basketball experience rooted in development, mentorship, and opportunity. Basketball serves as a universal language – one that builds confidence, connection, and discipline when paired with intentional coaching and support.

Built Locally. Dreaming Globally.

Overview
Global Dreams Academy is a 10-week youth basketball training and league program designed to prioritize player development, exposure, and mentorship over short-term results. The program combines weekly small-group skill training with structured league play in a positive, teaching-focused environment. This initiative is ideal for youth athletes who want to improve their fundamentals, gain confidence, learn the game the right way, and begin building habits that support elite long-term growth.

Program Elements
Duration: 10 weeks (March-May)
Participants: 40 athletes, grades 8-11
Teams: 5 teams (8 players per team)
Location: Andrews Academy Gym

Weekly Skill Training:
Small-group training sessions (4-5 athletes per session)
Focus areas include:
Ball handling
Shooting mechanics
Finishing
Defense
Decision-making
Training emphasized repetition, confidence, and game-like situations



Weekly League Play:
Structured Sunday games
Balanced round-robin schedule
Guaranteed playing time for all athletes
Teaching-focused environment with in-game coaching and feedback

Exposure & Player Visibility:
League games are filmed consistently throughout the season
Game footage is cut and edited into individual player clips
Players receive access to film for:
Skill review and learning
Highlight clips
Sharing with future coaches, trainers, or programs
This component introduces athletes to film-based learning and self-evaluation, an important tool at higher levels of basketball.

Mentorship & Guest Development Voices:
Select training sessions and league days feature guest coaches, trainers, and mentors
Guests may include:
Local high school and college coaches
Skill trainers
Former players and sport professionals
Mentorship focuses on:
Work habits and discipline
Coachability and leadership
Long-term athletic and personal development


Leadership

Founder & Program Director: Ian Ochieng
Global Dreams Academy was founded and led by Ian Ochieng, a basketball professional and youth development leader with experience across elite collegiate basketball environments, coaching, and community-based sport development. He brings a strong foundation of basketball expertise shaped by experience across elite competitive, operational, and developmental environments. His background includes working within high-level Division I basketball operations at the University of Michigan Men’s Basketball program, where he supported player development, daily team operations, and performance standards, as well as hands-on coaching experience with youth and student-athletes. In addition to collegiate experience, Ian has competed at the international level, having participated in training camp preparation with the Kenya National Basketball Program (Morans) in advance of the 2025 FIBA AfroBasket Qualifiers. Complementing his on-court background, Ian’s academic studies in sport management, leadership development, and community engagement informs a holistic, development-first approach. His leadership philosophy blends elite basketball standards with intentional mentorship, emphasizing fundamentals, discipline, character, and confidence as the foundation for long-term athlete growth.
